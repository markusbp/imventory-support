ImVentory Support

The visual inventory app that puts photos first.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I back up my data? Your inventory syncs automatically via iCloud when signed in. Photos are stored locally on your device.
  • What is the daily item limit? Free users can create up to 5 items per day. Upgrade to ImVentory PRO for unlimited item creation.
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device and select ImVentory PRO.
  • My data disappeared after updating. Make sure you are signed into the same iCloud account. If the issue persists, please contact us.

Troubleshooting

  • App won't open. Try force-quitting and reopening, or restart your device.
  • Photos not showing on another device? Make sure both devices are signed into the same iCloud account. If an item appears without its photo, try opening the app on the original device to allow syncing to complete.

Privacy Policy

ImVentory respects your privacy. All your inventory data and photos are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, data may sync across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. We do not operate our own servers and do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal data.

We do not collect analytics, usage data, or personal information of any kind.

If you subscribe to ImVentory PRO, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
